This is the rules of this game

You are going to guess a random number from 1 to 999. This will have to possibilities:
(1 Higher) The random number is higher then the number you guessed
(2 Lower) The random number is lower then the number you guessed

Based on this informasion you can guess again. You can guess as many times as you want.
The goal is to guess as few times as possible.
